Mirror & Rotate dialog box

Use the Mirror & Rotate dialog box to mirror the Art model around the X, Y, and Z axis or rotate it in 90-degree increments. By mirroring about the Z axis, a positive Art model becomes a cavity. Click the following drop-downs to view the examples.

Example 2: Mirror about Y

Example 3: Mirror about Z

NOTE
  • To create a mold for positive Art models, choose Mirror Z.
  • To create a mold for positive Art models that also include text, choose Mirror Z Reverse. This also reverses the text.
Parameters

Presets

  • Opens a list of options to: Automatically load the most recently used parameters.
  • Save your current dialog box settings to a file (.aps) in your Shared Mastercam 2025 folder.
  • Load a set of previously saved parameters by selecting it from the list.
  • Delete a set of saved parameters.
  • Reset all dialog box parameters to their initial default values.

Preset files are stored locally in a fixed directory structure (..\Art\Presets) in your Shared Mastercam 2025 folder.

NOTE

Networked colleagues can access your preset from your Shared Mastercam 2025 folder. If you send your preset files to Mastercam Art users who do not have access to your network, make sure to let the recipients know the correct folder where they should paste the file in their Mastercam installation. Your recipients can use Windows Explorer copy and paste functions to add your files to their local machine. Their file structure must match yours.

Mirror surface

Choose one of the following options.

  • Mirror X - Mirrors the active Art base surface around the horizontal center (X).
  • Mirror Y - Mirrors the active Art base surface around the vertical center (Y).
  • Mirror Z - Mirrors the active Art base surface around the Z axis, reversing it (positive to negative / negative to positive).
  • Mirror Z Reverse - If the Art base surface includes text, select this option (rather than Mirror Z) to also reverse the text. This option creates a good mold.

Rotate surface

Choose one of the following options.

  • 90+ - Rotates the active ABS 90 degrees in a counterclockwise direction around the surface center.
  • 90- - Rotates the active ABS 90 degrees in a clockwise direction around the surface center.
